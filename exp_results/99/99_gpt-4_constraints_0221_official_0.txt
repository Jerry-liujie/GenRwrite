1. Push down predicates: This rule can be applied when there are conditions on the columns of a table that can be used to filter the data before joining with other tables.
2. Use subquery to pre-calculate: This rule can be applied when there are complex calculations or transformations that can be done on the data before it is used in the main query.
3. Avoid join with date_dim table: This rule can be applied when the date_dim table is not used in the final result, and the conditions on date_dim can be transformed to conditions on other tables.
