2. Split UNION ALL subquery into two separate subqueries in the CTE: This can help the database optimizer to better understand the query and potentially improve performance.
3. Remove unnecessary DISTINCT keyword: If the columns being selected are already unique, there is no need to use the DISTINCT keyword, which can slow down the query.
