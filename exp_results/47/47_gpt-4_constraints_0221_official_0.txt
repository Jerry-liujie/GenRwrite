1. Use window functions to calculate lag and lead values in the same subquery: This rule can be applied when there are multiple window functions in different subqueries that can be combined into one subquery.
2. Remove unnecessary subquery: This rule can be applied when there are subqueries that do not contribute to the final result or their operations can be performed in the main query or other subqueries.
3. Avoid unnecessary case statement: This rule can be applied when there are case statements that are not needed because the conditions they check are already filtered out in other parts of the query.
