Remove unnecessary ROLLUP function in GROUP BY clause: The ROLLUP function is used to create subtotals and grand totals in the result set, but in this case, it's not necessary because we're not using the subtotals or grand totals in the outer query. Removing it can improve performance by reducing the amount of data that needs to be processed and grouped.
