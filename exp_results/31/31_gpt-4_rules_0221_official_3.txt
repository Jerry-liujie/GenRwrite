2. Filter data as early as possible: This reduces the amount of data that needs to be processed in the later stages of the query.
3. Replace multiple OR conditions with IN clause: This can improve performance by reducing the number of comparisons.
4. Avoid division by zero directly in WHERE clause: This can prevent errors and improve performance by avoiding unnecessary calculations.
