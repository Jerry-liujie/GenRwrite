4. Avoid repeated calculations: This rule can be applied when the same calculation is performed multiple times in a query, especially if the calculation is complex or involves a large amount of data.
5. Use IN clause for multiple OR conditions: This rule can be applied when there are multiple conditions in a WHERE clause that check if a column's value is equal to one of several specific values.
