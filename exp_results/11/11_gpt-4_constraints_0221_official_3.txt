3. Use JOIN instead of WHERE for joining tables in the main query: This rule can be applied when there are conditions in the WHERE clause that are used to join tables in the main query.
4. Move conditions from WHERE to ON in JOINs: This rule can be applied when there are conditions in the WHERE clause that can be moved to the ON clause in JOINs to filter data before the join operation.
